## Welcome!

A distant world awaits, where the powers of the mind are a force of history... and the future.

Please join us in visiting the Kitaran Sea Campaign, an ongoing [Dungeons & Dragons](http://www.wizards.com/dnd/) Campaign. We've been gaming for over 3 years now and are still going strong. Almost every bit of written information from the game is posted here on the site. Why? I'm a compulsive computer geek, most likely. In any case, enjoy!
