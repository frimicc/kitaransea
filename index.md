<h2 class="c2">Welcome!</h2>
<p class="c3">A distant world awaits, where the powers of the mind are a force of history... and the future.</p>
<p>Please join us in visiting the Kitaran Sea Campaign, an ongoing <a href="http://www.wizards.com/dnd/" target="_top">Dungeons &amp; Dragons</a> Campaign. We've been gaming for over 2 1/2 years now and are still going strong. Almost every bit of written information from the game is posted here on the site. Why? I'm a compulsive computer geek, most likely. In any case, enjoy!</p>
